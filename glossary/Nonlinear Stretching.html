Nonlinear Stretching

  • Nonlinear functions with a fixed form
  • Fewer parameters to adjust
  • Satisfying 0=fmin≤g≤fmax=L−10=f_{\min} \leq g \leq f_{\max} =L-1
  • Examples
    • Logarithmic transformation: g=blog(af+1)g=b \log(af +1)
      • Stretch dark region, suppress bright region
    • Exponential transformation: g=b(eaf−1)g=b(e^{af}-1)
      • Expand bright region
    • Power Law: g=afkg=af^k
      • K=2K=2: square law, similar to exponential
      • K=1/3K=1/3: cubic root, similar to logarithmic
