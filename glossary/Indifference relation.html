Indifference relation

Definition

Define indifference relation ≈i\approx_i, which expresses the fact that a player is indifferent between two possible outcomes x≈iy⇔x≿iy and y≿ixx \approx_i y \iff x \succsim_i y \text{ and } y \succsim_i x where ≿i\succsim_i denotes the preference relation.
