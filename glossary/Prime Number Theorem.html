Prime Number Theorem

Theorem (bound for x>55x > 55)

Let π(x)\pi(x) denote the number of primes less than some integer xx. For x>55x>55, xlogx≤π(x)≤xlogx−4\frac{x}{\log x} \leq \pi(x)\leq \frac{x}{\log x - 4}
