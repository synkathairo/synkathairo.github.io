Rayleigh quotient

LFor a fixed symmetric matrix AA, the normalized quadratic form xTAxxTx\frac{x^T A x}{x^T x} is called a Rayleigh quotient.

Given a positive definite matrix BB of the same size, the quantity xTAxxTBx\frac{x^T A x}{x^T Bx} is called a generalized Rayleigh quotient.
